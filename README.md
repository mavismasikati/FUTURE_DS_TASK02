# Customer Segmentation & Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project provides a comprehensive analysis of subscription-based customer data using **RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) customer segmentation**. Using **Power BI** and **DAX modeling**, I transformed a raw dataset of over 18,000 customers into an **interactive dashboard** that highlights key metrics, high-value customer segments, and areas for retention focus.

The dashboard enables data-driven decisions by visualizing critical KPIs such as **Total Sales**, **Churn Rate**, customer distribution across age groups, and RFM segments.

---

## 🚀 Business Questions Answered
1. **High-Value Identification:** How do we categorize our **18,484 total customers**? (Answered via RFM scoring into 11 segments)  
2. **Churn Crisis Mapping:** Why is the **Churn Rate at 74%**? (Visualized via the "Churned vs. Active" trend)  
3. **Revenue Concentration:** Which age group drives the most value? (Identified that the **55-64 age band contributes $11.5M**, the highest of any group)  
4. **Geographic Performance:** Which regions are leading in sales? (Visualized via the **Global Sales Map**)  

---

## 📈 Key Insights (Data-Specific)
* **The Retention Gap:** While the business acquired over **18.4K customers**, only **4.8K remain active**, resulting in a critical **74.12% churn rate**.  
* **Silver Economy Power:** Revenue is heavily concentrated in older demographics; customers aged **55+ account for over $19M** of the $29.36M total sales.  
* **Segment Priority:** The "**Champions**" and "**Loyal**" segments represent the core revenue safety net, while the "**At Risk**" and "**About to Sleep**" segments (totaling over **14K customers**) represent the primary target for win-back campaigns.

---

## 📝 Conclusion & Impact
This project successfully transformed a raw dataset of **18K+ records** into a **strategic retention tool**. By calculating a **$29.36M Total Sales benchmark** and identifying a **74% Churn Rate**, the dashboard provides a clear directive for the marketing team:  

> Pivot from broad acquisition to **high-value retention**, specifically targeting the "**At Risk**" segments within the **55-74 age demographics** to stabilize the revenue stream.
